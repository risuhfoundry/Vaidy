# Biomarker Aliases

This file is the editable biomarker normalization surface. Keep clinical
aliases here instead of adding them to Python code.

## Aliases

- hemoglobin: Hb | HGB | Haemoglobin | Hemoglobin
- wbc: WBC | TLC | Total Leukocyte Count | Total Leucocyte Count
- platelets: Platelet | PLT | Platelet Count | Platelets
- creatinine: Serum Creatinine | Creatinine
- alt: SGPT | ALT | Alanine Transaminase
- ast: SGOT | AST | Aspartate Transaminase
- tsh: TSH | Thyroid Stimulating Hormone
- vitamin_d: Vitamin D | 25-OH Vitamin D | 25 Hydroxy Vitamin D
- fasting_glucose: FBS | Fasting Blood Sugar | Fasting Glucose | Glucose Fasting | Glucose, Fasting (F), Plasma
- total_cholesterol: Total Cholesterol | Cholesterol Total
- hdl: HDL | HDL Cholesterol
- ldl: LDL | LDL Cholesterol
- ldl_direct: LDL Cholesterol Direct | LDL Cholesterol,Direct
- vldl: VLDL | VLDL Cholesterol
- non_hdl: Non-HDL Cholesterol | Non HDL Cholesterol
- triglycerides: Triglycerides | TG | Triglyceride
- apolipoprotein_b: Apolipoprotein B | Apo B | APOLIPOPROTEIN B (Apo B)
- hscrp: hsCRP | Cardio C-Reactive Protein | Cardio C-Reactive Protein (hsCRP), Serum | C-Reactive Protein High Sensitive
- lipoprotein_a: Lipoprotein(a) | Lp(a) | LIPOPROTEIN(a); Lp(a), SERUM
- troponin_i: Troponin I | Troponin- I | TROPONIN- I, SERUM HIGH SENSITIVE | hsTnI
- hba1c: HbA1c | Glycosylated Hemoglobin | HbA1c (Glycosylated Hemoglobin), Blood
- estimated_average_glucose: Estimated Average Glucose | eAG | Estimated average glucose (eAG)
